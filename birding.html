Birding

Bhigwan Bird Sanctuary !


Location :

Bhigwan is a town on the border of the Ahmednagar, Pune and Solapur districts in the Indian state of Maharashtra. Approximately 100 km from Pune.


Sightings :

At Bhigwan, ducks, herons, egrets, raptors and waders along with flocks of hundreds of flamingos are present. At times, 1,000-1,200 flamingos are seen including painted storks, barheaded geese and demoiselle cranes. Bhigwan hosts the fastest bird of the animal kingdom, the Peregrine Falcon.


Favourable Season and Cost :

Visit in migratory period ( November to March)
Rs 2,000/- (1 day....includes 2 hour Boat Ride, travel, breakfast, lunch, first aid )






Kumbhargaon Bird Watching !


Location :

It is located at about 100 km east from Pune city.


Sightings :

Coots, shovellers, spotbills, grebe, sandpipers, shanks, stilts, godwits in plenty. Breeding colonies of storks, herons, flamingos, ducks, gulls, terns


Favourable Season and Cost :

November to February
Rs 2,200/- (1 day....includes 2 hour Boat Ride, travel, breakfast, lunch, first aid )
