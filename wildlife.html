Pench Sanctuary !


Location :

Pench National Park is located in the heart of India- Madhya Pradesh and covers a total area of 758 sq.km.


Sightings :

Indian wild dog, jungle cat, wild boar, chital, jackal, hyena, and wolf. Deer, rhesus macaque, langur, gaur, sambar, and bison can also be seen in the herd in the forest.


Favourable Season and Cost :

March, April and May
Rs 25,000/- (4 Days 3 Nights....includes 4 safaris )

Jim Corbett National Park !


Location :

Jim Corbett National Park is the oldest national park in India and was established in 1936 as Hailey National Park to protect the endangered Bengal tiger. It is located in Nainital district and Pauri Garhwal district of Uttarakhand and was named after hunter and naturalist Jim Corbett.


Sightings :

Animals like wild elephants, Spotted Deer, Wild pigs, Hog Deer and Barking Deer. This zone is rich in the fauna species too with the animals like the Royal Bengal tiger, leopards, wild elephants, Nilgai, different species of Deer, wild bears and the various species of reptiles and birds.


Favourable Season and Cost :

The pleasant climate in the park makes it an ideal tourist spot all year-round, but the best time to visit Jim Corbett National Park is in winters.
Rs 50,000/- (6 Days 5 Nights....includes 6 morning and 5 night safaris )





Bankapura Peacock Sanctuary !


Location :

Bankapura Peacock Sanctuary is a well-known peacock sanctuary located in the State of Karnataka.


Sightings :

Bankpura Fort is home for not only for peafowl, but also a number of other birds like great-horned owl, babbler, magpie, robin, green bee eater, nightjar, spotted myna, paradise flycatcher, Indian robin, spotted dove, parakeets, kingfisher, grey hornbill, blue tailed bee eater, blacked winged kite, tailor bird etc.


Favourable Season and Cost :

Peacocks can be seen in large amount in winter season
Rs 20,000/- (2 Days 1 Nights....includes 2 morning and 1 night safaris )
